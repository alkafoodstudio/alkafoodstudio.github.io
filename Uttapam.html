Ingredients

  • 1 cup Suji (Semolina)

  • 1 Green Chili (Mirchi), finely chopped
  • Â½ cup Mixed Vegetables (onion, tomato, capsicum, carrot - finely chopped)
  • Â½ tsp Eno (fruit salt) or Â¼ tsp baking soda (optional)
  • 1 tsp Oil or Ghee (for cooking)

Instructions

  • In a bowl, mix suji, curd, salt, and water to make a thick batter. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  • Add chopped green chili and mixed vegetables to the batter.
  • Just before making uttapam, add Eno or baking soda and mix gently.
  • Heat a non-stick pan and grease it with oil or ghee.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread slightly.
  • Cover and cook on medium flame for 2-3 minutes until the bottom turns golden brown.
  • Flip carefully and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side.
  • Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
