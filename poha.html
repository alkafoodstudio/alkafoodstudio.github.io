Poha

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups Poha (Flattened Rice)
  • 1 medium Potato (Aloo), chopped
  • ¼ cup Peanuts
  • 1 medium Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • ⅓ tsp Rai (Mustard Seeds)
  • ½ tsp Green Chili (Mirchi), finely chopped
  • ½ tsp Haldi (Turmeric Powder)
  • 1 tsp Dhaniya Powder (Coriander Powder)
  • 1 tsp Salt (adjust to taste)
  • 6-7 Curry Leaves

Instructions

  • Rinse the poha under running water for a few seconds. Let it soften and set it aside.
  • Heat 3-4 tbsp oil in a pan on medium heat. Add the chopped potatoes and fry them until golden. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, fry the peanuts until crisp. Remove and set aside.
  • Reduce the oil to 1-1½ tbsp in the same pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add onions, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn soft.
  • Stir in turmeric powder, salt, and coriander powder.
  • Combine Everything- Add the fried potatoes and peanuts to the pan. Mix well.
  • Gently add the softened poha and stir everything together until evenly coated with spices.
  • Cover and let it cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat. Serve hot with fresh coriander and lemon wedges.
  • Enjoy your delicious Poha with tea or as a light breakfast!
