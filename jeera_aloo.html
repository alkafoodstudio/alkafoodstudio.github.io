Jeera Aloo

Ingredients

  • 3 large boiled potatoes
  • 1-2 tbsp oil
  • a pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 tsp Jeera (Cumin seeds)
  • 1 tsp Dhaniya Powder (Coriander powder)
  • ⅛ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • ¼ tsp Amchur (Dry Mango Powder) - optional
  • ¼ tsp Garam Masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish.

Instructions

  • Boil the potatoes in a pressure cooker for about 5 whistles. Once cooled, peel and cut them into chunks.
  • Heat 1-2 tablespoons of oil in a kadhai on medium heat.
  • Add a pinch of Hing and 1 teaspoon of Jeera, letting them sizzle for a few seconds.
  • Add 1 teaspoon of Dhaniya powder, ⅛ teaspoon of Red Chili powder, and ¼ teaspoon of Amchur (optional) to the pan. Stir the spices well for about 4-5 minutes.
  • Gently add the boiled potato chunks to the kadhai and stir to coat them evenly with the spices. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Sprinkle ¼ teaspoon of Garam Masala and give it a final stir.
  • Garnish with freshly chopped coriander and mint leaves.
