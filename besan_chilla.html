Besan Ka Chilla (Gram Flour Pancake)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • ½ cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • ½ inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil or ghee (for cooking each chilla)

Instructions

  • Prepare the batter In a mixing bowl, add besan (gram flour).
  • Gradually add water, whisking to avoid lumps. The batter should be smooth and slightly thick.
  • Add turmeric, red chili powder, carom seeds, cumin seeds, salt, onion, tomato, green chili, ginger, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  • Let the batter rest for 5–10 minutes.
  • Heat a non-stick pan or tava on medium flame. Lightly grease it with oil or ghee.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin pancake.
  • Cook for 2–3 minutes until the edges lift slightly, then drizzle some oil around the edges.
  • Flip the chilla and cook the other side for another 1–2 minutes until golden brown.
  • Serve hot with mint chutney, tomato ketchup, or curd.
