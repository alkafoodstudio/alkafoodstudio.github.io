Rice Pulao

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1 cup Green Peas (Matar)
  • ½ tsp Ghee
  • 4-5 pinches Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 tsp Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • ¼ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Salt (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups Water (double the rice quantity)

Instructions

  • Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it in 4 cups of water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pressure cooker, heat ½ tsp ghee on medium flame.
  • Add hing, jeera, and red chili powder. Sauté for a few seconds until aromatic.
  • Pour in 4 cups of water and add garam masala, salt, soaked rice, and green peas. Stir well.
  • Close the cooker lid and cook for 2 whistles on medium heat.
  • Let the pressure release naturally before opening the lid.
  • Fluff the Pulao gently with a fork and serve hot.
