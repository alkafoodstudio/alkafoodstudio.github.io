Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Chole (Chickpeas), soaked overnight
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1-inch Ginger
  • 1 Green Chili (optional)
  • 1 small piece Raw Turmeric (Kachi Haldi, optional)
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 4-5 pinches Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 tsp Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • 1 tsp Dhaniya Powder (Coriander Powder)
  • ½ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 cups Water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

  • Prepare the paste: Blend tomato, ginger, green chili, and raw turmeric in a mixer to make a smooth paste.
  • Heat 1 tbsp ghee in a pan. Add hing and jeera, and let them sizzle.
  • Add dhaniya powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
  • Pour in the tomato-ginger paste and sauté until the oil separates.
  • Add the soaked and washed chole, along with water. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook until the chole turn soft (pressure cook for 4-5 whistles).
  • Once cooked, add garam masala and give it a final stir.
  • Enjoy hot Chole with rice, roti, or bhature!
