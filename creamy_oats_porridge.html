Creamy Oats Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oats
  • ½ cup Water
  • 1 cup Milk (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp Ghee
  • Chopped Dry Fruits (Almonds, Cashews, Walnuts - as per taste)
  • Mixed Seeds (Flaxseeds, Chia, Pumpkin Seeds - optional)
  • Fresh Fruits (Banana, Apple, Berries - as per taste)

Instructions

  • In a pan, heat 1 tsp ghee and lightly roast the oats for 1-2 minutes until aromatic.
  • Add ½ cup water and 1 cup milk to the pan and stir well.
  • Simmer on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats soften and form a creamy texture.
  • Adjust the consistency by adding more milk or water as needed.
  • Once done, pour into a bowl and top with chopped dry fruits, seeds, and fresh fruits of your choice.
