Gravy Aloo

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • A pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 tsp Jeera (Cumin seeds)
  • ½ tsp Haldi (Turmeric powder)
  • ⅛ tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Dhaniya Powder (Coriander powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and peeled
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
  • Water as needed (enough to dip the potatoes)

Instructions

  • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pressure cooker on medium heat. Add a pinch of Hing and 1 teaspoon of Jeera. Let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  • Add ½ teaspoon of Haldi, ⅛ teaspoon of Red Chili Powder, and 1 teaspoon of dhaniya powder. Stir the spices well for a few seconds.
  • Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating from the masala.
  • Add the boiled potatoes and gently mash them a little, leaving some chunks intact. Stir to coat the potatoes with the masala.
  • Add salt as per taste, 1 teaspoon of Kasuri Methi, and enough water to cover the potatoes. Stir well.
  • Close the pressure cooker and cook for 5 whistles on medium heat.
  • Once the pressure is released, open the cooker and check the consistency of the gravy. Adjust the water if needed.
