Dhokla

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Curd (Dahi)
  • 2 tbsp Butter or 1 tbsp Pure Ghee
  • 1 cup Powdered Sugar
  • 1¼ cup Maida (All-Purpose Flour)
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Baking Soda (Meetha Soda)
  • ½ tsp Coffee Powder
  • A pinch of Salt

Instructions

  • In a bowl, mix curd, powdered sugar, and ghee/butter. Whisk well until the mixture becomes smooth and creamy like butter.
  • Using a sieve, sift together maida, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and coffee powder.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and mix well until smooth
  • Grease a bowl or cake tin with oil to prevent sticking. Pour the batter into the greased bowl.
  • Preheat a cooker on medium heat for 10 minutes (without whistle and rubber).
  • Place the cake batter bowl inside the cooker. Cover with a lid (without whistle and rubber) and cook on low flame for 35 minutes.
  • Insert a toothpick; if it comes out clean, the cake is done. Let it cool for a few minutes before demolding.
